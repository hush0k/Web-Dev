First day in KBTU

The first day at KBTU was both exciting and overwhelming. The campus buzzed with energy as students gathered for orientation. I met new people, explored the facilities, and received an introduction to university life. It was a mix of nervousness and curiosity, but overall, it felt like the start of a great journey.

Joining to Tumar orchestra

Joining the "Tumar" orchestra was a thrilling experience. I was welcomed by talented musicians and quickly became immersed in the rhythm of rehearsals. As a conductor, I felt both honored and nervous, but the atmosphere was supportive. It was the beginning of an incredible musical journey filled with growth.
